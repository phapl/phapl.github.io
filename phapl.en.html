PhaPl

PhaPl is a software to research and plot phase portraits of autonomous systems of 2 differential equations on a plane. PhaPl works as a site or as a local html-page that works offline.

Go to task input/choice below.

Download archive for local offline use (~9 MB to download, ~37 MB to be stored unpacked on disk).

This version was published on: 2018-06-06 01:19 +0300.

"Math Processing Error" everywhere should be a problem with cache. Refreshing of page bypassing cache should help (press Shift-F5 in Chrome or Control-F5 in Firefox).

You are welcome to send your suggestions and reports about errors / bugs / problems through GitHub.
